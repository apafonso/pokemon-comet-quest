Pokemon Commander Adventure

Pokemon Comet Quest

Pick Pikachu, Bulbasaur, Charmander, or Squirtle and commit to a full 100-level expedition. Each hero has a different movement power, ammo pickup, and combat style. Search every stage for comet parts, then defeat bosses like Meowth, Lapras, Charizard, and Mewtwo.

Mission

Explore 5 regions and clear 100 platforming stages.

Collect every comet part in each level to unlock the exit gate.

Every 20 levels the region changes, and every 10th stage is a boss lair with a core part.

Controls

A D or Left Right to move

W Up or Space to jump

X to use your Pokemon power

Enter to start, pause, or advance

N to begin a new campaign and unlock character choice
